What is this for?

This is intended for people who would like to post content without it being indexed by search engines (whether on social media, or otherwise). It's mainly to serve as a safeguard for people to speak freely without having to worry about people targeting them for specific key terms, such as civil rights movements or mentioning names of predators on social media.

How does it work?

This tool allows you to generate text using both cyrillic and greek characters, which look very similar to the English alphabet. This gives the benefit of making text normally readable, and preventing key terms from being easily searchable.

Do keep in mind that this isn't fool-proof; your generated text will be indexed, just not under the normal text. This is mainly to just prevent results under dragnet filters.

Does this send any data?

Nope! The data you input on this page isn't sent anywhere. However, it does require javascript enabled in order to work.
